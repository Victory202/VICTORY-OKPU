Aliexpress Dashboard Overview

  • Tools Used: Microsoft Excel
  • Github URL: Project Link

This AliExpress Sales and Profit Dashboard was developed using Power BI to analyze the performance of a retail branch or simulated "Superstore" from 2014 to 2017, providing a comprehensive view of sales, profit, and operational metrics.

Top-level metrics highlight overall performance, including total sales of $2.30M, total profit of $286.40K, and an average shipping time of 4 days, offering a quick snapshot of business health over the four-year period.

Sales and profit trends are visualized through dual line graphs, showing consistent growth from $484K in sales in 2014 to $733K in 2017, while profits nearly doubled from $49.5K to $93.4K during the same period.

Strategic breakdowns provide actionable insights: the top 10 sub-categories reveal Phones and Chairs as the leading performers, while other top categories include Storage, Tables, Binders, Machines, Accessories, Copiers, Bookcases, and Appliances.

Customer segmentation is visualized with a donut chart, showing Consumers contributing 51% of sales, Corporates 30%, and Home Office customers 19%, helping identify the most valuable customer groups.

Regional performance analysis indicates the West region as the dominant contributor to sales and profit, followed by the East, with Central and South regions contributing less to overall totals.

Additional operational insights include inventory data, noting 1,862 unique products, and fulfillment trends, with Standard Class identified as the most frequently chosen shipping method, supporting logistics and supply chain decision-making.

Overall, this dashboard transforms raw retail data into actionable insights, enabling stakeholders to optimize sales strategies, monitor product and customer performance, and improve overall profitability.
