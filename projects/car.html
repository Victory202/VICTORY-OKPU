Car Sales Dashboard Overview

  • Tools Used: Power Bi, Microsoft Excel

This Sales Dashboard was developed in Power BI for an automotive company to analyze annual performance metrics and sales distribution for a specific vehicle model.

Key performance indicators at the top of the dashboard provide a quick snapshot of business results, including total sales (746K), sales growth (22%), pre-sales (613K), and budget (428K).

Sales distribution and timing insights are presented through interactive visualizations: a monthly performance bar chart tracks sales volume from January to December, a donut chart breaks down sales types including Pre Book, Third Party, and Direct Sale, and a horizontal bar chart shows current year sales by category.

The dashboard interface allows users to filter data by year (2019â€“2022) and includes model selection tabs at the bottom to switch between different vehicle models, enabling detailed comparisons and focused analysis.

Visual storytelling is enhanced by a central high-quality image of the tracked vehicle model, emphasizing the connection between data and the product, while providing an engaging and user-friendly experience for stakeholders.

Overall, the dashboard transforms raw sales data into actionable insights, supporting strategic decision-making, monitoring growth trends, and optimizing sales performance across models and regions.
