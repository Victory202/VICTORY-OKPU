HR Dashboard

  • Tools Used: Tableau, Microsoft Excel
  • Github URL: Project Link

These dashboards present a comprehensive Human Resources (HR) analytics solution built using Tableau, consisting of both a high-level workforce overview and a detailed employee-level view.

The overview dashboard provides insights into workforce health and composition, showing 7,950 active employees, 8,950 total hires, and 1,000 terminations across the organization.

Departmental analysis highlights Operations as the largest department, followed by Sales and Customer Service, offering clarity on workforce distribution across business units.

Demographic insights reveal a balanced workforce with 54% male and 46% female employees, while education analysis shows the majority of employees holding Bachelor’s and Master’s degrees.

Compensation analysis demonstrates a strong relationship between education level and salary, with employees holding PhDs earning the highest average income, reaching up to $93K.

Age and salary distribution analysis indicates that most employees fall within the 35 to 45 age range, with salaries typically between $60K and $100K.

The detailed dashboard serves as an interactive employee lookup tool, allowing users to filter records by employee ID, role, geographic location, salary range, and employment status.

The employee-level view displays individual details such as employee name, education level, job title, work location, and length of service, supporting granular analysis and informed HR decision-making.
